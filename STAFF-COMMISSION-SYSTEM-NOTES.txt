STAFF COMMISSION SYSTEM

Added inside Admin > Reports:
- Staff Commission Report
- Default single item commission: P50 per item
- Default bundle commission: P50 per bundle
- Bundle counts as 1 commission item
- Export Commission CSV

How it calculates:
- Uses paid/completed orders in selected month
- Voided/cancelled orders are ignored
- Groups by cashier/staff name/email saved on POS sale
- Staff POS sales are tagged using the logged-in Firebase user email

Recommended workflow:
1. Staff logs in using staff account.
2. Staff uses Barcode POS.
3. Sales are saved with cashier email.
4. Admin opens Reports.
5. Select month.
6. Click Generate Commission.
7. Export CSV for payroll/commission payment.

You can change commission rates anytime in Reports:
- Single Item Commission
- Bundle Commission
